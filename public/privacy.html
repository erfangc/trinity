Privacy Policy for Trinity Prayer

Last Updated: Mar 22, 2025

Information We Collect

  • First Name Only: When submitting prayer intentions or responding to prayers, we collect and display only the first name of users.
  • Prayer Intentions: Users can submit their prayer requests, which are shared with others using the app.
  • Parish Name (Optional): When responding to prayer requests, users can optionally include their parish name, which will only be visible to the prayer requestor.

We do not collect or store any other personal data (such as email addresses, phone numbers, or location data).

How Your Information is Used

- Submitted prayer intentions will only display the first name of the requester and the content of their prayer request.
- Those responding to prayers will have their first name and (if entered) the parish name shared with the requester.

No other personal data is collected, stored, or shared.

Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your privacy:

  • No Data Storage: We do not retain, store, or share any personal information beyond the content willingly submitted within the app.
  • Once a request or response is displayed, no Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is stored elsewhere.

Third-Party Sharing

Trinity Prayer does not share, sell, or distribute any user information to third parties. All data interactions occur within the app and remain within the self-contained ecosystem.

Your Privacy Rights

Since no sensitive data is collected or stored:

  • You are not required to sign up, log in, or provide any personally identifiable information.
  • You are free to use the app without concerns about your data being tracked or harvested.
